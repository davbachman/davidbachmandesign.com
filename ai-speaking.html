Most engagements begin with a short conversation about audience, technical level, and institutional context.

What the work is for

The goal is not generic AI enthusiasm. It is to help people build a clear model of what current systems can do, where they fail, and how institutions can respond without confusion or panic.

  • Clarify capabilities and limits.
  • Design realistic policy and governance responses.
  • Build curricula that outlast the current news cycle.
  • Give educators and professionals actionable understanding.
